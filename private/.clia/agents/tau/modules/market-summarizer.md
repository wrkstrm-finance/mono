# Tau Module — Market Summarizer

Tau publishes end‑of‑day market and macro recaps and archives them for governance.

- Product: daily summaries highlighting unusual options flow, IV shifts, and sector dynamics.
- Stewardship: archive outputs (text/MD/JSON) for governance review and institutional memory.
- Prototype: `gymkhana-cli market-summary` (will be replaced by a scheduled task).
